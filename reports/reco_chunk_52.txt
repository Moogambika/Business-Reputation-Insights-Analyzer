**Business Improvement Recommendations**

Based on the customer feedback trends, the following practical recommendations aim to enhance the overall customer experience at Starbucks Church Street, Bengaluru.

### 1. **Service Quality**
* **Improve staff training**: Focus on enhancing the skills of staff, particularly in handling customer orders and providing excellent service even during peak hours.
* **Implement a customer feedback system**: Regularly collect feedback to identify areas for improvement and measure the effectiveness of implemented changes.

### 2. **Staff Behavior**
* **Enhance staff attitude and behavior**: Provide training on customer service skills, emphasizing the importance of being attentive, courteous, and helpful.
* **Recognize and reward good behavior**: Incentivize staff to maintain high standards of service by recognizing and rewarding good behavior.

### 3. **Facilities**
* **Maintain cleanliness of washrooms**: Ensure that washrooms are regularly cleaned and stocked with essentials like tissues.
* **Improve amenities**: Consider adding more amenities like parking options or partnering with nearby parking services to enhance the customer experience.

### 4. **Wait Time**
* **Optimize staffing during peak hours**: Ensure adequate staffing during busy periods to minimize wait times.
* **Implement an efficient order management system**: Streamline the ordering process to reduce wait times and improve the overall customer experience.

### 5. **Value for Money**
* **Maintain consistency in food and beverage quality**: Continue to offer high-quality products that meet customer expectations.
* **Provide value-added services**: Consider offering loyalty programs or promotions to enhance the overall value proposition.

### 6. **Digital Experience**
* **Improve the in-store digital experience**: Consider implementing technologies like mobile ordering and payment to enhance the customer experience.
* **Maintain reliable Wi-Fi**: Ensure that in-store Wi-Fi is reliable and fast to support customers who work or browse while enjoying their beverages.

**Specific Actions for Starbucks Church Street, Bengaluru**

1. **Address staff behavior issues**: Provide targeted training to staff at this location to address the lackadaisical attitude and improve customer service skills.
2. **Improve washroom cleanliness**: Ensure that washrooms are regularly cleaned and maintained to meet customer expectations.
