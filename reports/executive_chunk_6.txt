**Executive Summary**

**Customer Feelings:**

The overall sentiment is mixed, with some customers expressing extreme satisfaction and gratitude towards Apollo Hospitals, while others are disappointed and frustrated with their experience.

**Positive Feelings:**

- Customers are extremely satisfied with the care and expertise provided by Apollo's doctors, particularly Dr. R. Balaji, Dr. Mohammad Rehan Sayeed, and Dr. Kartik Natarajan.
- They appreciate the hospital's cleanliness, organization, and attention to detail.
- Patients praise the professionalism, compassion, and kindness of the hospital staff, including nurses, doctors, and administrative teams.
- The hospital's facilities, such as the Platinum Wing and Apollo Sindoori, are highly praised for their luxury and comfort.

**Negative Feelings:**

- Some customers are disappointed with the hospital's internal coordination and communication, which led to unnecessary confusion and delays.
- The lack of clear guidance and support for patients and families navigating the hospital's departments and processes is a recurring theme.
- Pricing transparency is a major concern, with some customers feeling shocked and exploited by the final costs of their procedures.
- While the hospital is well-maintained and clean, the valet parking and internal coordination outside the hospital are areas for improvement.

**Overall Satisfaction Level:**

The overall satisfaction level is around 60-70%, with some customers being extremely satisfied and others being disappointed. The hospital's strengths lie in its medical expertise, facilities, and staff, but it falls short in areas such as pricing transparency and internal coordination.

**Key Recurring Themes:**

1. **Pricing Transparency:** Customers are concerned about the lack of clear pricing structures and the potential for exorbitant costs.
2. **Internal Coordination:** The hospital's internal coordination and communication can be improved to reduce unnecessary confusion and delays.
3. **Staff Professionalism:** Customers praise the professionalism, compassion, and kindness of the hospital staff, but some feel that the hospital can do more to support patients and families.
4. **Facilities and Amenities:** The hospital's facilities, such as the Platinum Wing and Apollo Sindoori, are highly praised for their luxury and comfort.
