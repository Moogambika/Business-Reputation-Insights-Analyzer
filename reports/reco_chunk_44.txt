Based on the customer feedback trends, the following practical business improvement recommendations are made:

**Service Quality**

1. **Improve staff attentiveness**: Some customers reported lackadaisical attitudes from staff. Provide training to ensure staff are attentive, greet customers, and acknowledge their presence.
2. **Enhance order preparation**: Mistakes in order preparation were reported. Implement quality control checks to ensure orders are accurate and prepared efficiently.

**Staff Behavior**

1. **Train staff on customer service**: Employees were reported to be rude or blunt in their responses. Provide customer service training to ensure staff are friendly, helpful, and courteous.
2. **Empower staff to resolve issues**: Allow staff to resolve customer complaints or issues promptly, without escalating to managers unnecessarily.

**Facilities**

1. **Maintain cleanliness**: Some customers reported unclean washrooms. Ensure regular cleaning and maintenance of facilities, including washrooms.
2. **Restock essentials**: Provide adequate tissues and other essentials in washrooms to meet customer needs.

**Wait Time**

1. **Optimize staffing during peak hours**: Customers reported waiting for extended periods. Ensure adequate staffing during peak hours to minimize wait times.
2. **Streamline order preparation**: Implement efficient processes to minimize wait times, such as pre-preparing common orders or using technology to expedite order preparation.

**Value for Money**

1. **Maintain consistent quality**: Customers praised Starbucks' coffee quality. Continue to ensure consistency in the quality of beverages and food.

**Digital Experience**

1. **Improve bag storage and retrieval processes**: A customer reported difficulty retrieving a stored bag. Streamline the process for storing and retrieving customer bags to make it more efficient.

**Additional Recommendations**

1. **Parking options**: Consider providing parking options or partnering with nearby parking facilities to make it easier for customers to visit.
2. **People-watching**: The Church Street location was praised for its people-watching opportunities. Consider maintaining or enhancing the outdoor seating area to maximize this experience.

**Best Practices**

1. **Maintain the ambiance**: The Church Street location was praised for its serene ambiance, cozy vibes, and subtle lighting. Continue to maintain these elements to create a welcoming atmosphere.
2. **Staff recognition**: Recognize and reward staff for their role in delivering excellent customer experiences, such as the employee who assisted with retrieving a stored bag.

By implementing these recommendations, Starbucks can improve customer satisfaction, increase loyalty, and maintain its competitive edge.
